# Purpose

The Information Architecture defines how visitors navigate through Adesh Digital Presence.

The objective is to ensure every visitor quickly understands:

- Who Adesh Gaur is.
- What professional problems he solves.
- Why he is technically credible.
- What products and solutions he has built.
- How to establish professional contact.

The platform should guide users naturally from discovery to engagement.

---

# Design Philosophy

The platform is **not** designed as a traditional résumé website.

Instead, it is designed as a professional consulting platform that demonstrates expertise through products, technical leadership, verified credentials, and engineering practices.

Navigation should always answer one question:

> What information does the visitor need next?

Navigation should remain intuitive.

The Information Architecture should remain scalable for at least the next ten years.
