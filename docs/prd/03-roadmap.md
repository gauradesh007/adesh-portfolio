# Vision

Build a long-term professional platform that evolves from a professional website into a recognized source of enterprise integration knowledge, products, and thought leadership.

The roadmap focuses on sustainable growth over many years rather than short-term feature delivery.

# Long-Term Goals

The platform should become:

- The official professional website of Adesh Gaur
- A recognized source of Enterprise Integration knowledge
- A showcase for engineering products
- A trusted technical reference
- A destination for recruiters, architects, and technology leaders

---

# Success Metrics

The roadmap is successful when the platform:

- Demonstrates Enterprise Integration expertise
- Strengthens the Boomi professional brand
- Establishes AI engineering credibility
- Increases professional networking opportunities
- Attracts consulting opportunities
- Supports speaking engagements
- Encourages collaboration

---

# Guiding Principle

Every future enhancement should strengthen one or more of the following pillars:

- Enterprise Integration
- Boomi
- Healthcare Integration
- Technical Leadership
- Artificial Intelligence

Features that do not strengthen these pillars should not be prioritized.
