# Executive Summary

Adesh Digital Presence is the official professional platform of Adesh Gaur.

The platform is designed to establish a long-term professional brand that showcases Enterprise Integration Architecture, Boomi expertise, Healthcare Integration, API Architecture, Artificial Intelligence, Technical Leadership, and original engineering products.

Unlike a traditional résumé website, the platform demonstrates expertise through real products, verified certifications, technical publications, architecture practices, and production deployments.

The website should communicate credibility, professionalism, and technical depth while maintaining enterprise-level design quality.

---

The platform shall:

- Establish a strong professional brand.
- Increase visibility within the Enterprise Integration community.
- Increase recruiter engagement.

# Product Non-Goals

The platform is NOT intended to:

- Replace LinkedIn.
- Replace GitHub.
- Publish confidential employer information.
- Publish client names.
- Host proprietary documents.
- Become a social network.
- Become an online résumé repository.
