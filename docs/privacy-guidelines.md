# Privacy & Content Governance Guidelines

# Adesh Digital Presence

Version: 1.0

Status: Approved

Owner: Adesh Gaur

---

# Purpose

This document defines the privacy, confidentiality, security, and professional publishing standards for Adesh Digital Presence.

The objective is to establish a long-term governance policy that protects confidential information while allowing the platform to demonstrate professional expertise through original work, technical knowledge, and publicly verifiable achievements.

These guidelines apply to every page, article, product description, image, document, screenshot, diagram, presentation, and future publication.

---

# Core Principles

The platform follows five governing principles.

## Professional Integrity

Every published statement must be truthful, verifiable, and professionally responsible.

The platform should demonstrate expertise without exposing confidential information.

---

## Privacy First

Privacy takes precedence over marketing.

No content should expose information belonging to employers, clients, colleagues, partners, or confidential business relationships.

---

## Evidence Over Claims

Professional credibility should be demonstrated through:

- Public products
- Technical articles
- Verified certifications
- Open-source projects
- Public speaking
- Engineering practices

Avoid unsupported claims.

---

## Respect Intellectual Property

Only publish content that:

- was personally created,
- is publicly available,
- or may legally be shared.

Respect copyrights, licenses, trademarks, and employer intellectual property.

---

## Long-Term Reputation

Every publication should strengthen the long-term professional reputation of Adesh Gaur.

When uncertain whether something should be published:

Do not publish it.

---

# Information That Shall Never Be Published

The platform shall never disclose:

## Employer Information

- Internal employer documents
- Internal architecture
- Internal diagrams
- Internal standards
- Internal repositories
- Internal tools
- Internal communication

Employer names should generally not be emphasized unless intentionally disclosed in an appropriate public context.

---

## Client Information

Never publish:

- Client names
- Client logos
- Client screenshots
- Client architecture
- Client databases
- Client integrations
- Client documentation
- Client business processes
- Client metrics

Instead, use generalized descriptions such as:

- Large Healthcare Payer
- Enterprise Financial Organization
- Global Airline Technology Platform
- International Retail Organization

---

## Confidential Technical Information

Do not publish:

- Proprietary source code
- Configuration files
- Credentials
- API keys
- Passwords
- Internal endpoints
- VPN information
- Security configurations
- Network diagrams
- Infrastructure details belonging to employers or clients

---

## Personal Information

Do not publish:

- Personal phone number
- Home address
- Government identification
- Financial information
- Family information unrelated to professional activities

Professional contact should occur through:

```
adesh@gaurcloud.com
```

---

# Resume Policy

The résumé shall not be publicly downloadable.

Instead:

Visitors may request a résumé through the Resume Request workflow.

Benefits:

- Latest version is always shared.
- Distribution remains controlled.
- Professional engagement begins immediately.
- Sensitive details remain protected.

---

# Product Publishing Policy

Products published on the platform shall represent:

Original work

Learning projects

Independent engineering

Open-source work

Public demonstrations

Never publish products that reveal confidential employer implementations.

---

# Case Study Policy

Case studies should focus on:

- Engineering challenges
- Architecture thinking
- Design decisions
- Technical lessons
- Problem-solving approaches

Do not disclose:

- Customer identities
- Internal timelines
- Commercial information
- Business-sensitive metrics

---

# Image Policy

Allowed

- Professional profile photograph
- Product screenshots
- Public architecture diagrams
- Original illustrations
- Certification badges
- Public conference photos

Not Allowed

- Employer screenshots
- Client screenshots
- Internal dashboards
- Internal portals
- Proprietary diagrams
- Confidential documentation

---

# Certification Policy

Only publish certifications that:

- are personally earned,
- are publicly verifiable,
- or may legally be shared.

Whenever available, link directly to the official credential verification page.

Do not modify certification artwork or logos.

---

# Technical Content Policy

Articles should focus on:

Engineering principles

Architecture

Patterns

Best practices

Problem solving

Lessons learned

Avoid discussing confidential implementations.

Examples should be generalized.

---

# Artificial Intelligence Policy

The platform may discuss:

- Agentic AI
- RAG
- Enterprise AI
- Local AI
- AI engineering
- AI architecture

Do not publish confidential prompts, proprietary models, or employer-owned AI assets.

---

# External Links

Only link to trusted resources.

Examples:

- LinkedIn
- GitHub
- Official certification providers
- Official technology vendors
- Official documentation

Review external links periodically for validity.

---

# Content Review Checklist

Before publishing any new content, verify:

- No confidential information
- No client names
- No employer-sensitive material
- No proprietary diagrams
- No copyrighted content without permission
- Technical accuracy
- Professional tone
- Correct grammar
- Working links

If any item fails:

Do not publish.

# Governance

Every new page, article, product, and document shall comply with these guidelines.

If a future publication conflicts with this policy, this document takes precedence.

# Final Principle

Professional reputation is significantly more valuable than short-term visibility.

When deciding whether to publish information:

If there is uncertainty, choose privacy.

The long-term trust of employers, clients, colleagues, recruiters, and the professional community is the highest priority of Adesh Digital Presence.
